ILP(Initial Learning Program),

a 6-month long journey, is the perfect launchpad for new joiners to explore their talents in the corporate world. They get to be part of a team, manage projects, and solve real-time scenarios across the program. This hands-on experience prepares them to be ready to achieve business goals.

Leader's Desk

Message from our COO

" ILP is one of the flagship programs run by the Learning & Development team to train young talent from campus by following best practices and training methodologies to mould them into productive resources ready to be deployed in commercial projects within a time frame. This 6-month long training intervention is a real transition period for them to unlearn things that are not the best practices followed in the industry and re-learn new things that help them to perform better and smarter. Each trainee is getting ample opportunity to interact with Leadership, delivery experts, well-versed trainers, etc. As a fast-growing organization, we have lots of hope and expectations from these trainees as they are going to be key stakeholders in the years to come. Wishing them all a great career at Experion."

Suresh VP
